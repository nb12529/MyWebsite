Green Sea Turtles

Green sea turtles, like a lot of other animals, can't handle the heat from global warming. The temperature where their eggs are laid determines if there gonna be a boy or a girl. So with all this climate change messing up the temperatures, we have more female turtles hatching than males.
That means there aren't as many male green turtles out there.And that's bad news for the future population of these turtles because fewer males mean fewer of them to mate with females. It's gonna be tougher for them to grow in numbers.
