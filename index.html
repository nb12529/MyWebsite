The Cheetah

The number of cheetahs is rapidly declining due to climate change. Also, the numbers of cheetah's prey are dropping in certain areas. Cheetahs must change their diets as a result, and they now attack other creatures who inhabit the same habitat. These animals often don't have the same calories as the usual prey of cheetahs. Also, as temperatures rise, cheetah reproduction is reduced, which has an effect on the large cat's ability to reproduce. Because the numbers of cheetahs in the wild are quickly decreasing conservationists are urging people all across the world to support their efforts. Gaining knowledge about cheetahs and adding to conservation efforts are both some of the most effective ways to help cheetahs.
